Coders of the Caribbean

Overview

Revolutionizing cruise bookings with a user-centric platform for guests and agents, CodersOfTheCaribbean simplifies your sea adventure from start to finish.

Technical Highlights

  • Java: Chosen for its robustness and wide compatibility.
  • Swing UI: Basic yet intuitive and user-friendly for effortless navigation.
  • SQL Database: Ensures secure and efficient booking management.
